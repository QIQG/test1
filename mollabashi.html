Ali Mollabashi (علی ملاباشی)

I am currently an assistant professor at the School of Physics of IPM. Before joining IPM, I was a postdoc at Yukawa Institute for Theoretical Physics (Kyoto University), a Humboldt fellow at Max Planck Institute for Physics (Werner Heisenberg Institute, Munich), and a postdoctoral associate at the School of Physics of IPM. I have finished my graduate studies at Sharif University and IPM.

I am broadly interested in the intersection of quantum field theory, quantum information theory, and quantum gravity. My previous works are available at: INSPIRE , Google Scholar

Master and PhD projects are available. In case you are interested, please drop me a message at ali[DOT]mollabashi[AT]gmail[DOT]com.
